Our Story

Praveen Singh Chouhan serves as a Director of Babyraj Foundation, a registered not-for-profit entity in Rajasthan. Through structured charitable operations, the foundation supports animal welfare, shelter services, and gaushala programs.

As work expands, verified impact reports and compliance documents will be shared publicly to ensure accountability and donor confidence. We believe trust is built through openness and transparent operations.

Our Programs

Comprehensive care for every animal in need

Cow Rescue & Rehabilitation

Rescuing abandoned, injured, and elderly cows from streets and slaughterhouses. We provide lifetime care at our gaushalas with nutritious food and medical attention.

Learn More →

Dog Shelter & Adoption

Providing safe shelter, medical care, and adoption services for street dogs. Our shelter homes ensure every dog gets proper nutrition, vaccination, and love.

Learn More →

Medical Treatment

24/7 emergency veterinary services with state-of-the-art facilities. From surgeries to long-term treatment, we ensure every animal receives expert medical care.
