Babyraj Foundation

Babyraj Foundation is a registered not-for-profit private company incorporated on 10 August 2024.

Registered Office:
Nathdwara, Rajsamand District, Rajasthan, India

Corporate Identification Number (CIN):
U85500RJ2024NPL096593

The foundation was created to support gaushala operations, animal protection, and welfare-oriented programs through structured and lawful charitable activity.

Through grassroots efforts and community participation, we aim to provide shelter, treatment, and long-term care for animals in distress.

Praveen Singh Chouhan

Director, Babyraj Foundation

Praveen Singh Chouhan is listed in Indian corporate records as a Director of Babyraj Foundation, a not-for-profit organization incorporated in Rajasthan in August 2024.

The foundation is registered in Nathdwara, Rajsamand District, and operates with the objective of supporting gaushala services and animal welfare initiatives.

In public interviews and podcasts, Praveen has spoken about his involvement in establishing and operating gaushala facilities and coordinating rescue efforts during challenging periods for livestock and stray animals.

While some of these stories are drawn from community media platforms, the organization remains committed to transparency, responsible governance, and publishing verified impact information as operations grow.
