Our Work & Programs

Transforming lives through compassion and dedicated action

Core Initiatives

Under the leadership of Praveen Singh Chouhan, Babyraj Foundation has pioneered several life-saving programs.

Rescue Operations

Praveen Singh Chouhan has led countless rescue missions, saving street animals from accidents, abuse, and life-threatening conditions. Our emergency response team works 24/7 to ensure no call for help goes unanswered.

Establishing Three Gaushalas

We have successfully established and are operating three state-of-the-art Gaushalas in Rajasthan. These facilities provide a safe haven, nutritious food, and specialized medical care for over 1,000 cows and bulls.

Care for the Orphans

Beyond animal welfare, we are deeply committed to supporting orphaned children. We provide educational resources, clothing, and essential supplies to ensure they have the foundation for a bright future.
